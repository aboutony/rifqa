## Medical Disclaimer Requirements

Every release must preserve these positions:

- RIFQA does not diagnose.
- RIFQA does not prescribe medicine or dosage.
- RIFQA does not replace a clinician, emergency department, midwife, nurse, or licensed provider.
- User should follow clinician instructions over app or AI guidance.
- Urgent symptoms route to urgent care or clinician contact.
- Crisis support and urgent safety features are never paywalled.

## Legal Review Checklist

- PDPL lawful basis and consent flows reviewed.
- Data export/delete process reviewed.
- Cross-border processing reviewed if OpenAI, hosting, or analytics process data outside Saudi Arabia.
- B2B firewall language reviewed.
- Insurer/employer reporting cannot imply individual monitoring.
- Terms cover AI limitations and user-entered data accuracy.
