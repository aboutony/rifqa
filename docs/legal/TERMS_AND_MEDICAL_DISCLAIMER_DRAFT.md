# RIFQA / رفقة Terms and Medical Disclaimer Draft

Status: Draft for legal and clinical review  
Jurisdiction target: Kingdom of Saudi Arabia

## 1. Acceptance

By using RIFQA / رفقة, the user agrees to these terms and to the Privacy Policy. If the user does not agree, she should not use the app.

## 2. What RIFQA Provides

RIFQA provides maternal wellness support, tracking tools, reminders, AI-assisted educational guidance, relaxation recommendations, exercise prompts, and doctor-visit preparation tools.

## 3. Medical Disclaimer

RIFQA does not provide medical diagnosis, treatment, emergency response, prescriptions, or clinical decisions. RIFQA does not replace a doctor, midwife, nurse, emergency department, or licensed healthcare provider.

The user should always follow her doctor’s instructions. For exercise, doctor-provided instructions override any AI or app-generated suggestion.

## 4. Emergency and Urgent Symptoms

The user should seek urgent medical care or contact emergency services if she experiences symptoms such as:

- Bleeding.
- Severe pain.
- Clearly reduced or absent fetal movement.
- Fainting, severe dizziness, or difficulty breathing.
- Symptoms her clinician has told her are urgent.
- Feeling unsafe or at risk of self-harm.

RIFQA may show urgent guidance, but it is not an emergency service and cannot guarantee detection of every urgent situation.

## 5. AI Limitations

AI responses may be incomplete, inaccurate, or unsuitable for a particular medical situation. RIFQA uses safety rules and disclaimers, but the user remains responsible for seeking professional care.

AI must not be used to decide whether to delay urgent care.

## 6. Exercise Terms

Exercise recommendations are general wellness suggestions. The user should not start or continue exercise if:

- Her doctor advised against it.
- She has bleeding, severe pain, reduced fetal movement, dizziness, shortness of breath, or other concerning symptoms.
- She has a condition where her doctor said exercise may worsen her health.

Doctor-prescribed exercise plans should be followed over AI-generated suggestions.

## 7. Relaxation, Prayer, and Audio

Relaxation music, prayer, recitation, breathing audio, and playlists are supportive wellness tools. They do not treat or cure medical or mental health conditions.

## 8. User Data and User Content

The user is responsible for the accuracy of information she enters. Doctor summaries generated by RIFQA should be reviewed by the user before being shared.

## 9. Prohibited Use

The user must not use RIFQA to:

- Seek emergency treatment instead of contacting emergency services.
- Harass or harm others.
- Upload unlawful content.
- Attempt to bypass security controls.
- Use the app for medical decisions for someone else without appropriate authority.

## 10. Availability

RIFQA may be unavailable due to maintenance, technical failures, free-tier service limits, third-party outages, or internet connectivity.

## 11. Limitation of Liability

To the maximum extent permitted by applicable law, RIFQA is not liable for harm caused by misuse, delayed medical care, inaccurate user-entered information, third-party service outages, or reliance on AI output as medical advice.

## 12. Governing Law

These terms should be governed by the laws and regulations of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, subject to final legal review.

## 13. Required In-App Disclaimer Placement

Show a concise medical disclaimer in:

- Onboarding before health-data consent.
- Settings and privacy center.
- AI chat before or near the chat input.
- Emergency and urgent guidance flows.
- Exercise recommendation screens.

## 14. Legal and Clinical Review Required

This draft is not legal or medical advice. It must be reviewed by Saudi-qualified legal counsel and an appropriate clinical reviewer before public launch.
